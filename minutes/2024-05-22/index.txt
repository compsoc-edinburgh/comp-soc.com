<li>Emphasis on both practical and theoretical workshops for newcomers.</li>

<li><strong>BitDiscuss:</strong> Sessions for both beginners and experts involving discussing academic papers.</li>
<li><strong>Social Events:</strong> Regular social gatherings with pizza and pub visits.</li>
