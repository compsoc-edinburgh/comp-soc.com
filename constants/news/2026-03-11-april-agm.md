# April AGM!
**WHEN:** 2nd April 2026 - 1800-2100
**WHERE**: Informatics Forum G.07

Hey everyone, our AGM is coming up! If you want to have a say in the future of the society (and some free pizza as always) come along! If you'd like to run you can [submit a manifesto here](https://l.comp-soc.com/Manifestos2026AGM), and you'll want to prepare a speech in advance. You'll have **2 minutes** if you're running for an executive role, **1 minute** otherwise. We'll also be voting on an amended constitution. You can also run on the day!

You'll need to be a registered as a member to vote, which [you can do here](https://l.comp-soc.com/join).
Uploaded manifestos [can be found here.](https://comp-soc.com/news/206-03-11-april-agm-manifestos/)

\### Positions up for election:
- Executive:
	- President, Secretary, Treasurer
- Core committee:
	- Vice-president, Tech Secretary, Social Secretary, Social Media Officer, EDI Representative, Graphic Designer
- Representatives:
	- 2nd Year, 3rd Year, 4th Year, Masters, Old person

See you there!
