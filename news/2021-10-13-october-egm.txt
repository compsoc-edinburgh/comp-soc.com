<ul>
<li>
<p><strong>Vice President</strong>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Oversees working with our SIGs (Special Interest Groups).</li>
<li>Organises monthly STMUs (this involves pizza procurement and finding speakers).</li>
<li>Helps the President with everything.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>1st year representative</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Represents students in their year socially.</li>
<li>Promotes CompSoc events to students in their year, and communicates feedback from students to the rest of the Committee.</li>
<li>General involvement with organising society events. One may also choose to get involved with any sub-committees such as InfBall, Hack the Burgh, and more.</li>
<li><strong>You must be a UG1 student to be eligible for this role.</strong></li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>3rd year representative</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for communication between the Inf3 cohort and the society, and promotes events to the year.</li>
<li>General involvement with organising society events. One may also choose to get involved with any sub-committees such as InfBall, Hack the Burgh, and more.</li>
<li><strong>You must be a UG3 student to be eligible for this role.</strong></li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<h4>What you need to do</h4>
<ul>
<li>Send a short manifesto (max 200 words) to <a href="mailto:committee@comp-soc.com">committee@comp-soc.com</a> <strong>by 16:00 Thursday 28th October 2021</strong></li>
<li><a href="/join">Make sure you have CompSoc membership.</a> You cannot run for election without membership.</li>
<li>Prepare a speech! Up to 2 minutes for the vice president role, and 1 minute for the rep roles.</li>
</ul>
