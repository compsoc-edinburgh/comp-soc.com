c:T906,<h1>October STMU and EGM</h1>
<p><strong>When</strong>: 29th October- doors open at 5:30pm, STMU 6PM,  EGM 7:30pm
<strong>Where</strong>: Informatics Forum G.07</p>
<p>We'll be holding our next STMU and EGM soon! The EGM is where we elect unfilled roles for the rest of the year.</p>
<h2>STMU - "How to Tame the Elephant" - Bending Spoons</h2>
<p>Come hear from our sponsor <strong>Bending Spoons</strong> about the technical challenges faced and lessons learned from modernising note-taking app Evernote's decade-old codebase.
They'll discuss DevOps practices, building for reliability and improving the developer experience.</p>
<h2>EGM</h2>
<h3>Roles up for election</h3>
<ul>
<li>1st Year Rep
<ul>
<li>Responsible for communication between their year and the Society, and promotes events to their year.</li>
<li>Responsible for organising some of CompSoc's large projects and events, such as InfBall, HackTheBurgh or pwnedEd.</li>
<li>Gain an insight into the day-to-day of running CompSoc, and valuable experience in organising large scale society events.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>4th Year Rep - As above</li>
<li>Social Secretary - Be in charge of organising our social events!
<ul>
<li>Plan and execute all CompSoc social events</li>
<li>Be in charge of venue booking, logistics and promotion</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Junior Treasurer
<ul>
<li>Manage daily financial operations - including making reimbursements, maintaining budgets, and tracking transactions.</li>
<li>Gain hands-on experience in the financials of managing a Â£70,000+ balance sheet.</li>
<li>Obtain valuable insights into financial management and prepare for a potential future role as Treasurer.</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<h3>How do I run?</h3>
<p>To run for one of these roles:</p>
<ul>
<li><a href="https://l.comp-soc.com/ManifestosEGMOctober2025">Submit a manifesto here</a> (max 200 words!) - you can also run on the day but it's a good idea to write a manifesto. All manifestos should be sumbitted before the 29th.</li>
<li>Prepare a speech - these will be <strong>strictly 1 minute long</strong></li>
</ul>
<h3>Voting</h3>
<p>Voting will be in person and online - <strong>you'll need to have membership on EUSA</strong>, you can <a href="https://l.comp-soc.com/join">sign up for free here</a></p>
