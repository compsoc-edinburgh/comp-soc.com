14:Tb45,<p>During our last STMU of the year (6pm, 2nd April) we will be holding our AGM, and this means we need a new committee!</p>
<p>The positions available are as follows:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>President</strong>:
<ul>
<li>Responsible for the overall conduct of the society.</li>
<li>Willing to dedicate a lot of time.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><strong>Vice-President</strong>:
<ul>
<li>Organises monthly STMUs and other miscellaneous events with our sponsors.</li>
<li>Oversees working with our SIGs (Special Interest Groups).</li>
<li>Helps the society with everything.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><strong>Secretary</strong>:
<ul>
<li>Responsible for the administration of the society</li>
<li>Publicising events by newsletters, social media etc</li>
<li>Writing minutes &#x26; preparing agendas for AGM, EGM, and weekly meetings</li>
<li>Society merches</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><strong>Treasurer</strong>:
<ul>
<li>Accountable to the committee and members for the finances of the society</li>
<li>Reimburses society members for expenses</li>
<li>Writing annual financial report</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><strong>2nd year, 3rd year, Final year, Postgraduate and Old Person representatives</strong>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for communication between their years and the Society, and promotes events to their year.</li>
<li>Year reps are also responsible for any projects they take up throughout the year.</li>
<li>(Note that 1st year rep is elected at our EGM in October.)</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><strong>Technical Secretary</strong>:
<ul>
<li>Maintains technical services and in charge of technical setup for events.</li>
<li>Experience with server management is recommended.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><strong>Social Secretary</strong>:
<ul>
<li>Plans and organises our social events i.e. our pub nights, bowling, etc.</li>
<li>Sets up and heads the committee to organise the ball.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><strong>Graphic Designer</strong>:
<ul>
<li>Responsible for the creation of art, logos, and other designs required by the society for events, merchandise, and other promotional material.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><strong>EDI Officer</strong>:
<ul>
<li>Ensures that the society respects EDI (Equality, Diversity, &#x26; Inclusion) practices.</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<h4>Running for a position?</h4>
<ul>
<li>Send <a href="mailto:vicepresident@comp-soc.com">vicepresident@comp-soc.com</a> your manifesto (PDF of max 200 words) <strong>by Sunday 28th March 2021</strong> specifying your full name and the role in the title.</li>
<li>Prepare your speech. Length is 2 minutes for executive roles (President, VP, Secretary, Treasurer) and 1 minute for the other roles.</li>
</ul>
<p>There might also be votes for constitutional amendments. Visit the <a href="https://github.com/compsoc-edinburgh/constitution/pulls">GitHub Repository for the constitution</a> to propose or to see proposed changes.</p>
