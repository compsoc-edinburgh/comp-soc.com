c:Tc74,<p>During <a href="https://www.facebook.com/events/2546058098799848/">our last STMU of the year</a> (6pm, 20th March) we will be holding our AGM, and this means we need a new committee!</p>
<p>The positions available are as follows:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>President</strong>:
<ul>
<li>Should be someone with lots of free time (2nd years or 5th year students)</li>
<li>Responsible for the overall conduct of the society.</li>
<li>Should be good at delegating.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><strong>Vice-President</strong>:
<ul>
<li>Oversees working with our SIGs (Special Interest Groups).</li>
<li>Organises monthly STMUs (this involves pizza procurement and finding speakers).</li>
<li>Helps the President with everything.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><strong>Secretary</strong>:
<ul>
<li>Writes newsletters, handles social media, the annual report and minutes during meetings.</li>
<li>Also responsible for society apparel such as hoodies.</li>
<li>Knowing LaTeX is ideal but not necessary.</li>
<li>Nags people to get things done.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><strong>Treasurer</strong>:
<ul>
<li>In charge of finances.</li>
<li>Reimburses society members for expenses.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><strong>2nd year, 3rd year, Final year, Postgraduate and Old Person representatives</strong>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for communication between their years and the Society, and promotes events to their year.</li>
<li>Run for the year you will be in <em>next</em> year.</li>
<li>Year reps are also responsible for any projects they take up throughout the year.</li>
<li>(Our 1st year rep is elected at our EGM in October.)</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><strong>Technical Secretary</strong>:
<ul>
<li>Maintains technical services and in charge of technical setup for events.</li>
<li>Experience with server management is recommended.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><strong>Social Secretary</strong>:
<ul>
<li>Plans and organises our social events i.e. our pub nights, bowling, etc.</li>
<li>Books venues for our social events.</li>
<li>Sets up and heads the committee to organise the ball.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><strong>Hackathon Secretary</strong>:
<ul>
<li>In charge of the HackSIG subcommittee.</li>
<li>Organises hackathons throughout the year.</li>
<li>Finds suitable hackathons the society can attend and organises those events.</li>
<li>Limited to previous team members of Hack the Burgh.</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<h4>What you need to do:</h4>
<ul>
<li>Send <a href="mailto:vicepresident@comp-soc.com">vicepresident@comp-soc.com</a> your manifesto (PDF of max 200 words) <strong>by Monday 4pm 18th March 2019</strong> (Please mention "AGM" or "Manifesto" in the email title to make it easier to find)</li>
<li><a href="/join">Make sure you have CompSoc membership.</a> You cannot run for election without membership.</li>
<li>Prepare your speech. Length is 2 minutes for executive roles (President, VP, Secretary, Treasurer) and 1 minute for the other roles.</li>
</ul>
<p>There are also constitutional amendments, some required by EUSA, which can be found here: <a href="https://github.com/compsoc-edinburgh/constitution/pulls">https://github.com/compsoc-edinburgh/constitution/pulls</a>.</p>
<p>See you soon,</p>
