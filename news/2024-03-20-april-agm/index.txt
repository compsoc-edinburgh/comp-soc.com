d:T1020,<h3>Manifestos and the programme are now up!!</h3>
<p><a href="https://comp-soc.com/blog/2024/03/21/2024-agm-manifestos.html">Click here to view them.</a></p>
<h3>Original post below</h3>
<p>In the second half of our next STMU 18:00, 3rd of April, we will be holding our AGM where we vote for our new committee and any constitutional amendments.</p>
<p>The positions available are: President, Vice-President, Secretary, Treasurer, Technical Secretary, Social Secretary, Social Media Officer, Graphics Designer, 2nd Year Representative, 3rd Year Representative, 4th Year Representative, Old Person Representative, and EDI Rep.</p>
<p>We also have a new open position - <strong>Social Media officer</strong>!</p>
<p>See the following for descriptions of each position.</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p><strong>President</strong>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for the overall conduct of the society.</li>
<li>Responsible for facilitating communication between CompSoc and our sponsors.</li>
<li>Willing to dedicate a lot of time.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Vice-President</strong>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Organises monthly STMUs and other miscellaneous events with our sponsors.</li>
<li>Oversees working with our SIGs (Special Interest Groups).</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Secretary</strong>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for the administration of the society</li>
<li>Publicising events by newsletters, social media etc</li>
<li>Writing minutes &#x26; preparing agendas for AGM, EGM, and weekly meetings</li>
<li>Society merch</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Treasurer</strong>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Accountable to the committee and members for the finances of the society</li>
<li>Reimburses society members for expenses</li>
<li>Writing annual financial report</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>2nd year, 3rd year, 4th year, and Old Person representatives</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for communication between their years and the Society, and promotes events to their year.</li>
<li>Year reps are also responsible for any projects they take up throughout the year.</li>
<li>(Note that 1st year rep is elected at our EGM in October.)</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Technical Secretary</strong>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Maintains technical services and in charge of technical setup for events.</li>
<li>Experience with server management is recommended.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Social Secretary</strong>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Plans and organises our social events i.e. our pub nights, bowling, etc.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Social Media Officer (New role!)</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>In charge of social media accounts!</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Graphic Designer</strong>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for the creation of art, logos, and other designs required by the society for events, merchandise, and other promotional material.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>EDI Rep</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Implements initiatives to broaden inclusivity at CompSoc</li>
<li>Ensures communication and events at CompSoc are inclusive</li>
<li>Act as a confidential point-of-contact for EDI-related issues</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<h3>Want to run for a position?</h3>
<ul>
<li>Send <a href="mailto:committee@comp-soc.com">committee@comp-soc.com</a> your manifesto (PDF of max 200 words) <strong>by Tuesday, 2nd April 2024</strong> specifying your full name and the role in the title.</li>
<li>Prepare a speech. Length is 2 minutes for executive roles (President, VP, Secretary, Treasurer) and 1 minute for the other roles. You may get asked questions afterwards.</li>
</ul>
<h3>Constitutional Amendments</h3>
<p>We have a big consitution ammendment:</p>
<ol>
<li><a href="https://github.com/compsoc-edinburgh/constitution/pull/42">Constitution change</a></li>
</ol>
<p>Highlights:</p>
<ul>
<li>Updated list of committee roles</li>
<li>Provided more comprehensive guidelines for SIGs and SIG leaders</li>
<li>Detailed committee voting procedures</li>
<li>Added impeachment procedures</li>
<li>Added section on Discord team structure and policies</li>
<li>Added dispute resolution procedures</li>
<li>Added society dissolution guidelines</li>
</ul>
