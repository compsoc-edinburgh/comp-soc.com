14:Te8a,<h1>AGM details</h1>
<p>Our AGM is happening on April 2nd, at 18:00 in <strong>G.07</strong> of the Informatics Forum.</p>
<p>If you want to vote in the AGM, you need to <strong><a href="https://www.eusa.ed.ac.uk/activities/view/compsoc">make sure you have CompSoc membership</a></strong>.</p>

<p>We hope you think carefully about who you choose to elect tonight — please vote based on manifestos and speeches! Note that you may also vote for RON (reopen nominations) for all positions.</p>
