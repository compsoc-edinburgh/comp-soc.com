<h2><a href="https://www.irccloud.com/invite?channel=%23sigweb&#x26;hostname=irc.imaginarynet.uk&#x26;port=6697&#x26;ssl=1">Joining IRC</a></h2>
<p>Clients are needed to connect to an IRC server, but you do not need to create an account — these clients can be online
(e.g, KiwiIRC, or <a href="https://irccloud.com">IRCCloud</a>) or installed on your computer (HexChat).</p>
<p><a href="https://kiwiirc.com/client/irc.imaginarynet.uk:+6697#compsoc">You can pop in right away using KiwiIRC</a>, but if you already have
an IRC client installed on your device, just <a href="ircs://irc.imaginarynet.uk/#compsoc">click here</a> to join the server.</p>
<p>The folks over at the <a href="https://tardis.ed.ac.uk/">TARDIS project</a> also host a web client for you to use, called The Lounge (formerly <em>Shout</em>).
<a href="https://shout.tardis.ed.ac.uk">You can login or register on this server here</a>.</p>
<p>Be patient, with IRC you won't see any messages prior to the time you <em>connect</em>. Since Discord is bridged, you'll be able to see the chat history prior to your registration!</p>
